About Me

Hi, I'm John Lord Lacandula, a 4th-year college student. I started editing in 2017 when I first got my computer, and ever since then, I've been passionately learning to search and read articles and tutorials about editing. After many years of practicing, I became an editor in different school events and competitions. As I continue to work with other clients, I have earned enough money and had the funds to attend webinars to increase my expertise. After gaining experience in this industry, I decided to open this business as a freelance editor to help some students and people who need editors for their projects. I am not yet a professional editor, but I assure you that I can do my best in all projects that I will accept.
